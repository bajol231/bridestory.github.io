More About Company

Wedding Organizer is a special service that helps prospective brides & families in the planning and supervision of the implementation of a series of wedding parties in accordance with a predetermined schedule. WO also manages wedding events from the start of the marriage contract, traditional ceremonies, aids, catering, bridal makeup, documentation and others. In 1989 Ms. Yayah Rumdayah formed a Wedding Organizer which was named the WO bridestory.
